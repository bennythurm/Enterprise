About Us

At Italian Car Wash, we pride ourselves on providing top-notch car cleaning services with a touch of Italian flair. Our team is dedicated to ensuring your vehicle looks its best, using only the finest products and techniques.

Founded in 2017 we here at Italian Car Wash strive to provide the best service possible for you.

Where are we located

We are located in Roma, Italy, and we welcome customers from all over the region. Our facility is designed to provide a comfortable and efficient car washing experience.

Opening Times

We are open every day from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, ensuring you can get your car washed at your convenience.
